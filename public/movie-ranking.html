The best movies according to Jack

My top 3 movies of all time

Everything Everywhere All At Once

Incredible emotionally impactful movie dealing with themes of nilhism, absurdism and family trauma

The Shawshank Redemption

A tale of a man overcoming overwhelming obstacles despite being innocently locked up and having the patience to find justice

Memento

A time twisting non-linear tale that tells the story of a man who has short term memory loss and looking for the killer of his friend
